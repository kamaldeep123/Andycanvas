Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom shoe painting services

How long does a custom design take?

Most single-pair projects are completed within 2-3 weeks. Rush orders (1 week) are available for an additional 50% fee. Multiple pairs or complex designs may take 3-4 weeks.

What types of shoes can you paint?

We work with all types of footwear including sneakers, canvas shoes, leather shoes, boots, dress shoes, and more. Different materials may require different techniques and pricing.

What materials do you use?

We use only premium, professional-grade paints and sealers specifically designed for footwear. All materials are durable, flexible, and designed to last for years with proper care.

Can I provide my own design?

Absolutely! We love working with your ideas. You can provide sketches, reference images, or just describe your vision. We'll create a custom design proposal for your approval.

How do you handle shipping?

We provide secure, insured shipping both ways. You send us your shoes, we transform them, and ship them back safely. All shipping costs are included in your quote.

What's your satisfaction guarantee?

We offer 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with the result, we'll work with you to make it right or provide a full refund.

How much do custom shoes cost?

Pricing starts at $150 for basic custom artwork and varies based on complexity, materials, and design requirements. We'll provide a detailed quote after discussing your project.

Do you offer care instructions?

Yes! Every completed pair comes with detailed care instructions to help maintain the artwork's beauty and longevity. We also offer touch-up services if needed.
